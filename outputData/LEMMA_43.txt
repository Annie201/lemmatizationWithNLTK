Fresh Eating Apples are a Healthy Choice - Providing consumer with fresh and delicious tree fruit that are safe and wholesome is priority one at Rainier Fruit Company.
Our role in making sure that the fruit we supply is safe for our consumer and their family is an important one.
We store our produce in temperature-controlled atmosphere to maintain both quality and freshness.
We maintain the cold chain from storage through delivery to reduce spoilage due to excessive heat.
We pack our fruit in a clean and healthy environment to reduce the risk of contamination.
We train our supervisors, manager and production staff on the latest technique on Food Safety and Food Handling.
We submit to independent third party inspections, evaluation and grading of our Food Safety program and Production Facilities.
We group variety for maximum shelf life.
We accurately monitor and control temperature and humidity of truck and shipping containers.
We move shipping container carefully.
Rough or excessive handling can cause damage to almost any fresh produce.
