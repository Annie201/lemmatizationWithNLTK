XIAOTITLE Celebrate Apple Month with Your Favourite BC Tree Fruit | BC Tree Fruits.
KELOWNA, BC - This February, the largest producer of local apple in Western Canada, BC Tree Fruits, is inviting Canadians to celebrate Apple Month by loading up on local apple varieties.
We might not always equate February with fruit season; however BC Tree Fruits apple are available in multiple delicious varieties.
Apple Month encourages supporting local grower while adhering to Doctor s order of an apple a day!
The 2012 Apple Month also mark a milestone for BC Tree Fruits a it is the Okanagan organization s 75th Anniversary.
75 year ago in 1936 a group of dedicated farmer came together with a goal to provide the finest tree fruit worldwide.
From that first planted seed grew a co-operative of over 700 producer that continues today.
To commemorate Apple Month this February, BC Tree Fruits is launching it first nationwide contest – The Artful Apple.
Artful Apple asks fruit fan to decorate their favourite variety of BC Tree Fruits apple and upload the work of art to share.
The public will vote for their favourite Artful Apple.
The individual with the winning apple will be awarded an exlusive Okanagan vacation package for four.
Full prizing and detail at www.artfulapple.com . Apple Month is an initiative that date back to 1960 a a way to celebrate and promote locally grown fruit,  said Rick Austin, Director of Sales and Marketing for BC Tree Fruits.
Some consumer think the winter month leave no opportunity for fresh fruit or quality fruit; however we are proud to have multiple apple variety in the produce sale and Apple Month aid in spreading this awareness, Austin adds.
During Apple Month, shopper will notice additional display of BC Tree Fruits apple variety available at this time of year, including Royal Gala, Ambrosia, McIntosh, Spartan, Red Delicious, Golden Delicious, Granny Smith and Fuji.
Consumers will be able to sample apple in store during the month.
