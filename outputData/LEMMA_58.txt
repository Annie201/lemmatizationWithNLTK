XIAOTITLE Washington State Fruit - Apple.
Washington designated the apple a the official state fruit in 1989.
Washington produce about 42 percent of the apple grown in the United States (and 60% of those grown for fresh consumption).
The majority of apple production occurs east of the Cascade Mountains in the central area of the state.
During 1997-2001 Washingtons apple production averaged about 123 millon bushel per year.
Many variety of apple are grown in Washington state inclucing Red Delicious,Golden Delicious, Fuji, Gala, Granny Smith, Braeburn, Jonagold, Pink Lady, and Cameo.
About 100 packing house store, sort, box and ship Washington Apples nearly all 12 month of the year.
Washington grower are able to produce apple using organic method relatively successfully because of low disease pressure from apple scab and the absence of some key pest (such a Plum Curculio and Apple Maggot).
